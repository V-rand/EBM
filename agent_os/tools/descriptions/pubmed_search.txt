搜索 PubMed 生物医学文献（MEDLINE + PubMed Central）。

示例用法：
- `pubmed_search(query="metformin diabetes cardiovascular", article_type="systematic_review")`
- `pubmed_search(query="SGLT-2 inhibitor MACE", article_type="rct")`
- `pubmed_search(query="COVID vaccine efficacy", clinical_query="therapy")`
- `pubmed_search(pmid="34101387")` — 查看完整元数据 + grade_readiness
- `pubmed_search(pmids="34101387 37937763")` — 批量查看
- `pubmed_search(author="Fauci AS", year="2020")`
- `pubmed_search(query="breast cancer", article_type="guideline", year="2023")`
- `pubmed_search(mesh="Diabetes Mellitus, Type 2", article_type="meta_analysis")`

参数: query(搜索词), article_type(systematic_review|rct|meta_analysis|guideline|review|observational|case_report), clinical_query(therapy|diagnosis|prognosis|etiology), pmid, pmids, author, title, journal, year, mesh, max_results, clinical_query_sensitivity(broad|narrow)
